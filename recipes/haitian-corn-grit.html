Haitian Corn Grit




Description

The Haitian corn grit is the corn flour cereal from the maize. It is being cooked more or less like rice but with much more water and a a much higher temperature. Basically, hot water with or without spices is enough. Then it is served with all kind of sauce, beans soup, sea food etc... Sometimes it is also mixed with beans for making it more nutritious.

Some people use very few quantity of water making it hard like a bread so they can directly take it with the hands for eating it. The choice of accompaniments is endless.

Ingredients

  • Corn flour
  • Meat, soup, vegetables, sea food etc.. (we assume this is ready, pour focus is the corn grit)
  • Spices (Condiments))
  • Salt (a littele bit, protect your health)
  • Oil (a littele bit, protect your health)

Steps

  1. Pour a bit of oil in your recipient
  2. Add a bit of condiments (not that much)
  3. Add watter (two times the volume of your congrit)
  4. Once the water start boiling, add the corn grit
  5. Stir the corn flour continuously until it is cook / you can consider reducign the fire level if it is too muche
  6. Between 20 to 30 minutes (depending on the fire level, your recipient etc..) your corn grit should be ready to be eaten
